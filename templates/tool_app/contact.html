Frequently Asked Questions

Are your tools really free?

Yes! All our tools are completely free to use with no hidden fees or subscriptions.

Do I need to create an account?

No registration required! You can use most tools immediately without creating an account.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we process files securely and don't store your data unnecessarily. Files are processed temporarily and then deleted.

What file formats do you support?

We support a wide range of formats including PDF, Word, images (JPG, PNG, etc.), audio, and video files.

Can I suggest new tools?

Absolutely! Use the form to suggest new features or email us at features@toolbox-project.com.
